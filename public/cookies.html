Cookie Policy

Short version: this site doesn't use tracking or advertising cookies, so there's no consent banner. Here's exactly what is stored in your browser.

What this site stores

NameTypePurposeLifetime
theme Local storage Remembers your light/dark mode choice Until you clear browser data
chat_token Session storage Keeps your Live Chat conversation connected to the right session Cleared when you close the tab
access_token / refresh_token Cookie (HttpOnly) Keeps the site owner signed into the admin panel 15 minutes / 30 days — only ever set when logging into /admin

What's not here

No Google Analytics, no advertising pixels, no third-party tracking scripts, and nothing that follows you to other sites. That's also why you won't see a cookie consent banner — there's genuinely nothing here that needs your consent under most privacy laws.

Managing or clearing these

The theme and chat_token entries can be cleared anytime from your browser's site data settings, with no effect beyond resetting your color mode or starting a fresh chat conversation. The admin session cookies only ever apply if you're logged into the admin panel — regular visitors never receive them.
