Discovery settings
Runs on a cron (database/run_beacon_discovery.php): searches the keywords below via Serper, runs new results through Beacon, and sends you a digest (Slack and/or email, whichever is configured under Settings) of anything qualified — Beacon can't post back to Reddit/X/LinkedIn itself, so this is a digest for you to act on, not an auto-reply. Serper only returns a short snippet per result, not the full post, so treat qualified results as a lead worth opening, not a final verdict.
Google ranks old threads highest for evergreen phrases — without this, sweeps return posts from years ago that nobody is hiring from. Tighter is fewer but fresher.
What discovery is costing

Serper bills 1 credit per search; every new result costs an AI call. Failed scores cost a call too and buy nothing — if that number is climbing, a provider is rate-limiting you.
